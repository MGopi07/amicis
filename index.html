WHAT WE OFFER

Amicis Industries specializes in the production and supply of chlorine dioxide for various industries, ensuring versatility and effectiveness across numerous applications. Our expertise spans the following sectors:

Water Treatment

  • Providing chlorine dioxide generators for water treatment in different application sites to eliminate harmful pathogens, control algae and improve water quality.

Agriculture and Livestock

  • Providing disinfection programs for fruits, environment, water systems, equipment, barns, etc., to avoid disease transmission and reduce agricultural losses.

Food and Beverage

  • Ensuring top hygiene and safety standards in food and beverage processing with chlorine dioxide generators for sanitizing equipment, surfaces, and products.

Healthcare

  • Offering chlorine dioxide disinfection solutions for hospitals to eliminate bad odors, kill pathogens, reduce the risk of infections and maintain sterile environments.

Environmental Disinfection

  • Utilizing chlorine dioxide for environmental disinfection for a variety of application scenarios, eliminating odors, allergens, viruses, bacteria, etc and promoting sustainability.

Industrial Applications

  • Supplying chlorine dioxide generators for industrial uses, such as pulp and paper manufacturing, oil and gas processing, cooling tower and so on.
