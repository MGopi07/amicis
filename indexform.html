CATTLE FARMING ADVICE TO Prepare the cattle

The 'safety' tips for applying a pest control to a bovine

Deworming is common in breeding (cattle and sheep). Simple gestures and adapted equipment allow you to work safely.

Prepare the material

If the animal preparation is important, that of the material is just as important.

Equip yourself with protective clothing (gloves, long sleeves…)

Apply the product

For a good application, distribute the required dose on the dorso-lumbar line, from the withers to the base of the tail, without interruption.

Do not forget

Respect the prescription of your vet.

Identify the animals treated and fill in the waiting times in the health log.

Ask your veterinarian for advice.

POULTRY ADVICE

Tip-1 – The farmers should not purchase expensive land; always remember Economical / cost effective land must be planned for Poultry farm. Land in rural areas is always preferred over land in urban areas as they are very expensive.

POULTRY ADVICE

Tip-2 –The farm shall not be located at least 100 meters from any major water drinking source and 500 meters from any other livestock / poultry farm enterprise.

POULTRY ADVICE

Tip-3 – Farmer should not construct farm on main Road as too much noise of vehicles will create disturbance to your birds resulting stress and less output.

POULTRY ADVICE

Tip-4 – Ensure the availability of basic amenities like quality water & electricity supply preferably three phase.

POULTRY ADVICE

Tip-5 – Proper drainage / outlet for collection and discharge shall be provided for storm runoff / waste discharges (possibly explore the tenet of rain water harvesting).

POULTRY ADVICE

Tip-6 – No obstruction shall be created for any water course within the farm or outside the farm boundary.
